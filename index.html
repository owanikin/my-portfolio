Who I am

Technologist. Entrepreneur. Humanist.

Ifeoluwa is a budding entrepreneur who has exhibited remarkable commitment and genuine interest in using technology to help shape business decisions and improve commercial processes in sub-Saharan Africa.

In addition, he successfully organized and curated TEDxEastLegon themed “The Future We See” which brought notable speakers from around the world to spark thought provoking ideas and compel new perspectives.
